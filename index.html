About

I am a senior Computer Science Major and I attend Oregon State University-Cascades in Bend, Oregon. I have always been passionate about technology ever since I was little child. I want to help future organizations/companies create innovative software that better the lives of everyday people. I am an experienced System Administrator, Help Desk Supervisor, and Information Security Specialist of 8 years. I got my experience from the U.S. Active Duty Army. I have the the discipline, motivation, and the initiative that it takes to work in a fast-paced environment. So, that is why I am pursuing my Bachelors in Computer Science Applied Software Engineering. Besides having a passion for Technology, I spend my time in the great outdoors of Oregon. If I am not doing that, I like to practice makeup artistry as it gives me an outlet to be creative as well as create beautiful art.
